About Us

Crafting Timeless Celebrations Since 2010

Alliance to Honeymoon is Chennai's premier wedding planning company specializing in authentic South Indian celebrations. Our team of dedicated professionals brings over 15 years of experience in creating memorable weddings that honor tradition while embracing contemporary elegance.

We understand that your wedding is one of life's most significant milestones. That's why we meticulously handle every detail—from venue selection and decoration to catering and photography—ensuring a seamless and stress-free experience for you and your families.
